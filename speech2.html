2. Types of tourism

There are too many reasons to travel, and that´s why there are too many types of tourism. But, did Bolivia is among your options of countries to visit? In this essay you can see a few places you can visit in one of the most important cities in Bolivia. La Paz is the second city in Bolivia, in there you can see and feel the diversity of Bolivian people, one of the most recognizable characteristics from this the place. This city has beautiful places and landscapes to offer their tourists.
Now, the focus will be on two specific places of La Paz, for two different types of tourists. The first place is for the adventure ones, and it consists on climb the Illimani, this snowy mountain is part of the Cordillera Real and is one of the main attractions of the city. The second recommended place is Senda Verde, for the tourists who love the contact with nature and animals. This place is a life refuge located in the warmest area of the city, where you can rest outdoor and be in contact with animals (native of Bolivia in its majority).
Visiting La Paz city has many positive aspects. One of these is aspects is the diversity of places and climate (depending on the locale). It doesn’t matter what kind of person visits this city, always can find something interesting to do. Another aspect is that traveling in Bolivia, not only in La Paz, is cheaper than other countries in South America (according to Bárbara Muñoz). So tourists can have a good time, paying little.
Nevertheless, there are also some aspects that must be taken into account to prevent any difficulty in visiting this city. One of the most recognized is the height of the city, due to La Paz is at 3.600 meters above sea level (more or less, depending the locale). About the places cited before, climbing the Illimani could be dangerous and risky, because one can slip and fall from the mountain. Carrying oxygen is important too, to support the lack of air. For the other side, Senda Verde doesn’t have the comfort of the conventional hotels. Besides there are many bugs that can bite you, owing to the climate of the place.
Concluding, La Paz city is very diverse, and there are activities for any kind of person. There are many activities to do outdoors, and in the city itself, there are activities that can be done anywhere in the world, like go shopping or go to the movies. But the recommendation to the tourists who want to visit La Paz one day, is that they can visit places that are some distance from the city. As well as the places mentioned above, there are many more, such as Tiwanaku, the Sun Island, the Moon Valley, among others.

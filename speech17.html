17.TRAVELING



INTRODUCTION
Traveling for some people have different purposes, someone travels to gain knowledge and experience, someone travels for pleasure, someone travels for business purposes, etc. We can learn many things of travelling, like to trade and commerce things, customs, culture, languages, history, etc. in just one place that someone could visit. Also, traveling is the most satisfying thing to invest your money, because it brings you many benefits and experiences.
POSITIVE
The main reasons are a lot of why a person take the opportunity of traveling. The most exciting is to know about new places, new cultures and the most important new experiences. It doesn’t matter what’s the reason, you just live new things that you can tell then like a story, to all your relatives. The gladdest moment in human life, is a departure into unknown lands and discover why the world it’s so varied and so rich in many things.
POSITIVE
Traveling open your mind and leaves you speechless of everything you see when you are in some new place. At the beginning, will be the scariest, most liberating, life changing experience of your life, and it’s more fun if you travel with some friends or relatives. Also, travelling tends to increase all the human emotions, and all the most de-stressing things to do when you know something new.
NEGATIVE
But traveling has some negative things too, like the investment of money that could be a huge amount of money depending the place you are travelling. Another one, if you don’t know at least one of the languages that communicate the world if you travel out of your continent, it’s necessary to know English, Spanish or Chinese mandarin. Also, if you aren’t an adventure person that could walk for hours and prefer to stay at the hotel, travel isn’t for you.
CONCLUSION
Concluding, travel infects all with happiness. It’s the freedom of being on the road with no deadlines, or the everyday discovery of new destinations, foods, cultures and friends. But travel I a great way to find a balance in your life, with the result that you will become happier and also you will be a health person. Travel in just a few words, is the most exciting thing that you could ever had.

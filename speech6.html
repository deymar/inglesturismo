Benefits of travelling

Nowadays tourism is one of the most popular ways of learning, knowing about cultures, traditions and customs around the world. Travelling can teach you more than any university course. You must not care about money because there are different options for traveling free. You can learn a lot about yourself through travelling. You can observe how you feel being far from your country.

One of the benefits is that it teaches you about the world, another benefit is that it teaches you about your homeland. If you travel, you will not only learn about foreign cultures. When you return home, you will find out what makes your culture unique. Another benefit is that you can learn about yourself, you will be able to observe how you react in completely new situations, also you can test your language and definitely you will not the same person after returning home. Another benefit is that it makes you more tolerant because if you travel, you will meet new people that have different behavior and you have to accept these differences. One of the most important benefit is it makes you independent because you will realize that you can survive without all that help that is always available for you at home.

There are others benefits, for example many scientific studies have discovered that traveling or taking a regular holiday is good for your health, I will mention some benefits for improving your health . You can sleep better, lower blood pressure, lower stress hormones and increased life expectancy.

Traveling can improve your performance in your career for example open minded, communication, planning, adaptability and teamwork skills.

Nonetheless I am going to tell you some of the disadvantages, Non-travelers have no idea what you have experienced, so do not expect too much empathy when it comes to feeling wanderlust Building up a place in your head as being amazing, and then seeing it in real life can be really disappointing. Some people even develop anxiety and depression from that experience.

To sum up, my personal advice is you should travel a lot, you should learn, know and live new cultures, traditions, and customs around the world. Check out my infographic below for some of the most interesting statistics about the benefits of traveling.

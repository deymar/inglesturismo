15. Types of tourism

Today the tourism sector aims to cater to the needs and preferences of all types of tourism.
Today we have a plethora of tourism types.

Quick Fact

Cultural Tourism
This kind of tourism involves the culture of a particular country or region.
The concept of cultural tourism encompasses things, such as history of a given region, the lifestyle of people in a particular geographical locale, architecture, oral traditions, festivals and so on
Ecotourism
Ecotourism is a very broad category that involves a socially responsible travel to virtually undisturbed areas of natural beauty
Genealogy tourism
This is a very interesting form of tourism in which tourist travel to the lands of their ancestors in quest of their roots
LGBT Tourism

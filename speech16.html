16. Hotel Trends

Space tourism, closer than ever

Our ancestors looked at the skies and imagined than one day the man would travel out of this world. This dream now will be real, thanks to "Space-x" an enterprise laddered by Elon Musk, they begin the develop of the project for send a one passenger to the moon. Japanese billionaire and online fashion tycoon Yusaku Maezawa, 42 years old, announced the next: "I choose to go to the Moon." Definitely a huge challenge...

Develop the space tourism in my opinion is the most exciting trend in tourism, because, all people want to explore the unknown. Therefore, more and more people will be interesting about the space, more children will be interesting in engineering and astronomy. The experience of travel to the space isn't comparable with another one because is totally different than other types of travel. It could be a new way for people to interact.

On the other side we have the cons of space tourism, is really but really, really easy than something goes wrong, a bad calculus or bad circumstances (weather, temperature, cosmic powder, etc), could cause the explosion of the spacecraft, also this could cause fear in people to travel out of Earth. Another disadvantage could be the cost of travel, it’s too expensive and many people couldn't access but the solution could be the "VR reality".

Somebody asked to Musk how much it would cost to develop the BFR (spacecraft), and he said $5 billion (low end, $2 billion, high end $10 billion). Look, I’m a dilettante, but that seems low? The Space Launch System, NASA’s similar rocket, has cost about $2 billion to develop. Other projects to become reality the space tourism is "Virgin Galactic" who requires an up-front deposit of over £200,000 ($250,000) for a ‘seat to space’ and ‘membership of the Future Astronaut community’ for begin the develop.

Space travel or space tourism is a fairly large field that has not yet been completely studied, for this reason we need to be adventurous and bet on the unknown, supporting this trend. It was a dream of our ancestors that we are about to meet, currently only the rich people can have the opportunity to travel but as always, as more people are interested the prices will go down.

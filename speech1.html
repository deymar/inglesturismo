1.Tourism trends of 21 century


Travel truble


Venecia

Over time, people did a lot of things to feel comfortable and good with themselves. If you read some of the history of humanity, you can find ways of act that entire societies applied in their daily live to achieve that. Maybe we can say that some want exercise power over others just to feel good, others need to serve for not die and they agreed with that. Nowadays people talk about doing something that make them happy, so we can mention: personal realization, traveling around the world, have a good relationship, be include optimally in a social group. I want to rescue the statement: traveling around the world for accomplish happiness. I could say that it is possible, if we understand that happiness arises from the relationship between neurogenesis, neurotransmitters and both: physical and mental health, but this is not all. A lot of travel companies offers us many packages that we could access and buy a ticket that bring us any place of the world, this according to the questions “where I want to go?” or “where I need to go?”. First of all, neurogenesis and neuroplasticity means that the brain open new neuronal pathways through the creation of new neurons and the increase of speed of neuronal connections (neurotransmitters). Nussbaum cited in Noel (2014) says that traveling is a great way to experience neuroplasticity because the brain has new experiences and learns from this new place, this actions let the brain process on a deep level. The benefits of traveling it's not only at the brain level, but also physical. To explain more the physical benefit, we have to talk about the biological factors between health and happiness. Dfarhud, Malmir, and Khanahmadi (2014) say that to achieve a good health we need external and internal factors. For example, if you’re in a trip (external factor), your brain understands the new situation and codify it in brain connections (internal factor), that let the person feel happy. But the human body is awesome, and the process still going on, because feeling mentally good reflects in feelling physically good. Even we can call this a happiness cycle.

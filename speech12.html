12.Tourism and its influence in the knowledge society

Since ancient times tourism has become a first-choice option, for men, travel and have the opportunity to meet other destinations such as the sea for business reasons, work, social, family and even health, are a sufficient motivation to enjoy a great relaxation and a total personal renewal, which will allow the traveler to integrate with more encouragement, to their daily life.

In these modern times, where technology grows the giant steps, tourism is experiencing a great development internationally and over the years, has become one of the most important pillars of the world economy, without forgetting that It is an industry that generates jobs at all levels, also promoting large hotel chains, tour operators, statistics and companies specialized in the area, improving the quality of its services and offering tourists a comfortable stay, a wide range of options providing innovative alternatives that undoubtedly, will make it easier and more pleasant to visit tourists who are looking for a different alternative to enjoy a fun vacation at sea, alone or accompanied by their family group (gastronomy, tours of the city or country, events musical and cultural and related).
